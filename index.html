Welcome to The TaylorBowl Wiki! The official online encyclopedia for the Discord game TaylorBowl. Play now!

TaylorBowl is a game centered around voting for a preferred Taylor Swift song of two chosen, with additional minigames, an economy, and more. New to TaylorBowl? Check out the Introduction page!
