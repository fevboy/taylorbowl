Perpetua
This page is under construction. We are currently looking for images to add to it. Please DM ev on Discord if you have any images that may be worthwhile. Thank you!
Perpetua are a rare currency used to perform Evocations. They are a permanent currency; their name is derived from the word perpetual. They are the rarest and most difficult to obtain currency, and are sometimes referred to colloquially as “perps”.
Perpetua are only obtained through using the Invocation Crystallize.

The command !crystallize consumes 32 Ephemera to reduce the invoker's Ephemera Level by 16 and to create a Perpetua.

There is no limit to the number of Perpetua a user can hold, but the sheer difficulty of obtaining them makes this a non-issue. To date, the record for most Perpetua Crystallized is shared by Ivi and malene, at four Perpetua.

As the cost of Ephemera Levels and Ephemera constantly fluctuate, there is no fixed number of credits required to create a Perpetua. However, it is possible to estimate such a cost based on Active Credits:

$$\text{Exceed cost}=0.002\cdot\text{Active credits}\cdot\sum_{i=1}^{16}i=0.272\cdot\text{Active credits} \\ \text{Ephemera cost}=32\cdot 0.0007\cdot\text{Active credits}=0.0224\cdot\text{Active credits} \\ \text{Total cost}=0.272\cdot\text{Active credits}+0.0224\cdot\text{Active credits}=0.2944\cdot\text{Active credits}$$

It is also possible to calculate the cost of a Perpetua in Potentia as the value of Potentia are also tied to the number of Active Credits and they have a 1-to-1 conversion with Ephemera:

$$\text{Number of Potentia}=\cfrac{0.272\cdot\text{Active credits}}{0.0005\cdot\text{Active credits}}+32=576$$

And since each Potentia requires $1 USD to purchase, it follows that the value of a single Perpetua is $576 USD.
Perpetua are chiefly used to perform Evocations, which are extremely powerful commands that can affect many aspects of TaylorBowl.

In the command !perpetua, the description of Evocations is hidden if the command user has no Perpetua. Holding at least 1 Perpetua will reveal all Evocations. However, the number of Perpetua required to execute Collapse is still hidden, though it has 3 digits, so is between 100 and 999.

Due to the difficulty involved in obtaining Perpetua, only the Evocations that have been successfully used will be described below. Note that some Evocations are not coded into BowlBot yet – these are Pariah, Martyr, and Collapse. However, given that no user has yet attained more than 4 Perpetua, there is little need at the present moment to do so.
